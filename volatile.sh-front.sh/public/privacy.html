> PRIVACY_POLICY

Zero-knowledge means we have zero knowledge of your data.

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

volatile.sh is designed as a zero-knowledge service. This means we technically cannot access your secrets because they are encrypted in your browser before they reach our servers. The decryption key is stored in the URL fragment (the part after #), which browsers never send to servers.

2. What We Store

We store only the following encrypted data:

  • Encrypted ciphertext - Your secret encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Initialization Vector (IV) - 12 bytes required for decryption
  • Expiration timestamp - When the secret should be auto-deleted

Important: Without the decryption key (which we never have), this data is mathematically impossible to decrypt.

3. What We Don't Store

  • Plaintext secrets (your secret never leaves your browser unencrypted)
  • Encryption keys (kept in the URL fragment, never sent to servers)
  • User accounts or personal identifiers (no registration required)
  • IP addresses linked to secrets (hashed for rate limiting only)
  • Access logs or read receipts

4. Operational Data

For service operation, we temporarily store:

  • Rate limit counters - Hashed IP addresses (SHA-256) stored in RAM only
  • Request metrics - Anonymous aggregate data for capacity planning

This data is ephemeral (RAM-only), never persisted to disk, and automatically deleted.

5. Data Retention

  • Secrets: Automatically deleted after first read OR when TTL expires (5min-7days)
  • Rate limit data: Stored for 1 hour maximum in RAM
  • No backups: Deleted secrets cannot be recovered

6. Data Residency

volatile.sh is hosted on Cloudflare's global network. Data is stored in Cloudflare Durable Objects, which replicate within geographic regions for reliability. We do not control specific data center locations—this is managed by Cloudflare's infrastructure.

7. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Right to Access

Due to the zero-knowledge architecture, we cannot provide access to your secrets because we do not have them. Only someone with the full link (including the fragment) can access a secret.

Right to Deletion

Secrets are automatically deleted immediately after reading or when the TTL expires. There is no mechanism to request deletion because it happens automatically by design.

Right to Portability

Not applicable. Your data is encrypted and we do not have access to it. The link itself (which you control) is the only "port" needed.

Right to Opt-Out (CCPA)

We do not sell personal information. We have no personal information to sell. The service is anonymous by design.

8. Children's Privacy

volatile.sh does not collect personal information from anyone. The service is anonymous and requires no registration. We do not knowingly target children under 13.

9. Cookies and Tracking

volatile.sh does not use cookies for tracking. We may use essential cookies for technical functionality (e.g., Cloudflare's security features). We do not use:

  • Advertising tracking
  • Analytics cookies
  • Marketing cookies
  • Third-party tracking scripts

10. Browser Security Reminders

While we protect your data on our servers, your security also depends on your environment:

  • Browser extensions may access page content
  • Screen recording software can capture your screen
  • Link previews in chat apps may send URLs to external servers
  • Shared devices may have malware or keyloggers

11. Data Breaches

In the event of a server breach, the attacker would only have access to encrypted ciphertext without decryption keys. AES-256-GCM is considered unbreakable with current technology. However, we will notify users via the website if a significant incident occurs.

12. Legal Demands

If we receive a subpoena, warrant, or other legal demand:

  • We cannot provide decrypted secrets (we don't have them)
  • We cannot provide access logs (we don't keep them)
  • We can only provide encrypted ciphertext (useless without keys)
  • We will challenge overbroad requests to the extent permitted by law
